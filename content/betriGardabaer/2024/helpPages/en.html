A Better Garðabær 2024

  • Between 8-20 th of May 2024, Garðabær residents born in 2009 or earlier can vote on various proposed projects in an electronic vote.
  • Voters will vote on proposals for ISK 100 million, to be implemented over the next two years.
  • Residents can “heart" one of their selected proposals to double its weight, i.e. giving it two votes instead of one.
  • People may vote as often as they like while voting is open, but keep in mind that only the latest vote counts.
  • Illustrations of proposals on the voting page do not necessarily represent their final form.
  • The location of proposals is subject to change.
  • Votes are cast on a website where residents identify themselves in a secure manner and all votes are encrypted. This ensures that votes cannot be linked to individuals.
  • This is not a local referendum in the sense of Article 68 of the Local Government Act no. 138/2011.
  • Note that in order to vote, you have to have a digital certificate on your mobile phone, or an Íslykill identification. Further information on digital certificates can be found at www.audkenni.is and on Íslykill identification at www.island.is/islykill
  • Votes will be cast on the website kosning2024.gardabaer.is
  • Voting assistance is provided by the Garðabær’s service centre at Garðabær City Hall at Garðatorg 7, at the Garðabær Municipal Library at Garðatorg 7 and at the library branch in Álftanes.
  • Further information on the direct democracy project “A Better Garðabær", along with detailed instructions and voting rules, can be found on Garðabær’s website, gardabaer.is, or by calling Garðabær’s service centre, tel. 525-8500.
  • Questions and comments can be e-mailed to gardabaer@gardabaer.is during the voting period.
